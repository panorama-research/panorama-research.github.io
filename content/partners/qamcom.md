Qamcom was formed in 2001 with the ambition to be an agile catalyzer for advanced technology transfer between academia and industry. Qamcom has strong expertise in these areas covering everything from HW, embedded SW to system level e.g. communication systems, signal processing, image processing, AI, radar and functional safety.
